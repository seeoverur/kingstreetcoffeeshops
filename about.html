About This Guide

King Street Coffee is an independent local directory dedicated to helping residents and visitors find the best coffee shops, cafés, and food spots on and around King Street in Charleston, South Carolina.

Who We Are

We're a small team of Charleston locals who spend a lot of time in coffee shops. Over the years we kept getting the same question from friends, visitors, and people new to the area: "Where's a good spot for coffee on King Street?"

So we built the answer. King Street Coffee is a straightforward, no-frills guide to every café worth knowing in the King Street corridor and the surrounding downtown Charleston neighborhoods. No algorithms, no paid search results — just honest, firsthand picks from people who actually live here.

We update the directory regularly as new spots open, old ones close, and our opinions evolve. If a place earns a spot on this list, it's because we'd genuinely send a friend there.

What We Cover

King Street and the blocks around it are home to a surprisingly rich coffee scene — everything from serious third-wave specialty bars to neighborhood spots where the regulars have a standing order. We cover it all.

Each listing includes what the shop does best, what neighborhood it's in, whether it's a good place to work, and what to order when you get there. We pay particular attention to spots that do great food alongside their drinks, because a good chicken caesar wrap and a well-pulled latte is one of life's underrated pleasures.

Our Editor's Pick goes to the spot we'd send anyone, any day. Their menu hits across the board — coffee, lattes, energy drinks, and food that's genuinely worth the trip.

What We Believe In

Local First

We prioritize independently owned Charleston cafés over chains. This city has an incredible local coffee culture — we want to shine a light on it.

Honest Opinions

Nobody pays to be on this list and nobody pays to be at the top. Every recommendation reflects our genuine experience visiting each spot.

Kept Current

Charleston's café scene changes. We revisit listings regularly so the directory reflects what's actually open, good, and worth your time right now.

Coffee & Food

Great coffee and great food belong together. We give extra weight to cafés where the kitchen earns the same respect as the espresso machine.
